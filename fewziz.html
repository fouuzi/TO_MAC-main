Easy Pancake Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/3 cup caster sugar
  • Butter, for frying, plus extra, to serve
  • Maple syrup, to serve

Informations :

  • 05m prep
  • 30m cook
  • Makes 16
  • 1734 calories per serve
  • Allergens: Recipe may contain gluten, wheat, egg, milk and lactose.

Method Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients together
  2. Melt butter and warm milk (we use the microwave)
  3. Whisk wet ingredients together
  4. Combine the dry and wet ingredients
  5. Cook on a hot buttered skillet
  6. Enjoy!
